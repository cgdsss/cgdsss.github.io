About

Mr. Guangda Chen is a fourth-year Ph.D. student (BA17011, advised by Prof. Xiaoping Chen) in the Multi-Agent Systems Laboratory at the School of Computer Science and Technology, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). He received a bachelor's degree of management from China Medical University in 2014. His research focuses on mobile robot navigation in dynamic and crowded environments, and interests include Reinforcement Learning, Computer Vision and Calibration.
